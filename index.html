EXPLORE NATURE !

All children are eager to connect with nature; given a chance, they develop a strong love for the earth and everything upon it. Their senses are wide open to the great and the small as children watch moon and stars, they are awed to catch a glimpse of our planets place in the universe, while tiny creatures and objects grip their imaginations too. Perhaps the miniature size of ladybird, tadpole or acorn rouses feelings of sympathy in young children. Often a child will stop to watch an ant or examine a pebble that an adult would not even notice.
Gentleness toward plants and animals can extend to understanding for fellow humans as a child develops.

WATERFALL

A waterfall is any point in a river or stream where water flows over a vertical drop or a series of steep drops. Waterfalls also occur where meltwater drops over the edge of a tabular iceberg or ice shelf. Waterfalls can be formed in several ways, but the most common method of formation is that a river courses over a top layer of resistant bedrock before falling onto softer rock, which erodes faster, leading to an increasingly high fall. Waterfalls have been studied for their impact on species living in and around them.

Waterfalls can pose major barriers to travel. Canals are sometimes built as a method to go around them, other times things must be physically carried around or a railway built.In 1885, the geographer George Chisholm wrote that, "The most signal example of the effect of waterfalls and rapids in retarding the development of civilisation is undoubtedly presented by the continent of Africa, the 'darkness' of which is almost entirely due to this cause.

BEACH VIEW

A beach view is a captivating blend of nature's most serene and awe-inspiring elements. Picture this: the sun rising or setting, casting a golden or rosy hue across the horizon, where the sky meets the endless expanse of the ocean. The gentle waves roll in rhythmically, their white foam kissing the sandy shore. The beach itself is a canvas of soft, powdery sand, sometimes dotted with shells and smooth pebbles. Palm trees sway gracefully in the breeze, their leaves rustling like nature's own wind chimes. The air is filled with the refreshing scent of saltwater and the distant cries of seagulls, adding to the symphony of sounds that include the soothing crash of waves and the occasional laughter of beachgoers.

As you take in this scene, you feel a sense of peace and tranquility wash over you, the worries of daily life melting away with each breath of the fresh, ocean breeze. Whether you're basking in the sun, building sandcastles, or simply gazing out at the horizon, a beach view is a timeless reminder of nature's beauty and the simple joys of life.

OCEAN VIEW

The beauty of an ocean view is a mesmerizing and tranquil experience that captivates the senses. Picture standing on the shore, where the vast expanse of water stretches endlessly to meet the horizon. The rhythmic sound of waves crashing gently against the shore creates a soothing symphony that calms the mind. The water itself can vary in color, from deep, mysterious blues to vibrant, crystal-clear turquoise, each hue reflecting the sky above. As the sun rises or sets, the ocean transforms into a canvas of breathtaking colors, with shades of orange, pink, and purple dancing on the surface, creating a magical and serene atmosphere.

The salty breeze carries the refreshing scent of the sea, mingling with the occasional calls of seagulls flying overhead. The sight of the sun shimmering on the water's surface, creating a sparkling effect, is both enchanting and peaceful. The gentle sway of the waves can be mesmerizing, drawing you into a state of relaxation and awe. An ocean view offers a sense of vastness and freedom, a reminder of the beauty and power of nature. It's a place where you can find solace, reflect on life's wonders, and feel a deep connection to the world around you.
